Services / Products:

Our container shipping services are designed to provide reliable and efficient transportation of cargo worldwide. We operate a fleet of over 700 vessels and offer a range of container types to suit different cargo types and requirements. Our online platform, Maersk.com, makes it easy for customers to book shipments, track cargo, and manage their supply chain operations in real-time.

 

In addition to our container shipping services, we also offer freight forwarding services, including air and sea freight, customs brokerage, and warehousing. Our end-to-end supply chain management solutions are designed to help customers optimize their supply chain operations and reduce costs.

 

Maersk Line also offers intermodal transport solutions, refrigerated cargo services, and a range of trade finance solutions to help customers manage their cash flow and reduce financial risk in international trade. Our commitment to sustainability and innovation is at the heart of everything we do, and we are constantly investing in new technologies and processes to improve our services and reduce our environmental impact.

 

We are proud to be a trusted partner for customers in over 120 countries, and we look forward to working with you to meet your shipping and logistics needs.
